My career as an enterprise IT blogger

I write about enterprise mobility and end user computing at BrianMadden.com, a website founded by my brother, Brian Madden. BrianMadden.com is a part of TechTarget.

You can find me on Twitter and on LinkedIn

Here's a list of all of the articles I've written for BrianMadden.com and other TechTarget sites.

Topics I cover include:

  • Enterprise mobility management (mobile device management, mobile app management, etc.)
  • Windows 10 and new ways of managing desktops
  • The consumerization of IT (aka Shadow IT, Rogue IT, FUIT, etc.)
  • Identity and access management
  • How end user computing is changing as more mobile devices, mobile apps, cloud apps, and SaaS

In 2013 I published a book, Enterprise Mobility Management: Everything you need to know about MDM, MAM, and BYOD.

The book is still good way to get a foundation in EMM, but since it's a few years old it's missing a ton of recent developments. I have a list of supplemental resources and articles that I update every few months.

My music

I currently play in the Bay Area Rainbow Symphony, a volunteer orchestra in San Francisco.
