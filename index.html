Jack Madden

Enterprise IT marketer and blogger

I'm currently on the product marketing team for VMware Workspace ONE.

You can find me on Twitter and on LinkedIn.

From 2011 to 2020, I was a blogger, analyst, and executive editor at TechTarget, writing for the now-retired website BrianMadden.com, covering enterprise mobility management, "consumerization of IT" trends, identity management, desktop virtualization, and all things related to end-user computing.

In 2013 I published a book, Enterprise Mobility Management: Everything you need to know about MDM, MAM, and BYOD.

Music

I play bass and tenor trombone in the San Francisco Bay area. Prior to my blogging and marketing career, I studied bass trombone performance at the Oberlin Conservatory of Music and Duquesne University.

I currently play in the Bay Area Rainbow Symphony, a community orchestra in San Francisco. I also play with a six-piece dance band that gigs around the Bay area, and do some musical theater pit orchestra work.
